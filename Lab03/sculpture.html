Different types of Clay

Air Dry Clay

  1. Easiest for beginners
  2. Can be found at most all craft stores
  3. Does not need to be oven-baked or kiln-fired

Oven Bake Clay

  1. Similar to air dry clay, but requires to be baked to fully dry
  2. Many people use this for jewlery making
  3. Easy to do at home if you have an oven, or art ovens are usually fairly cheap

Kiln-Fired Clay

  1. Must be filn-fired to comletely dry
  2. Can be glazed to make foodsafe
  3. Many art studios will allow you to ue their kiln for a fee

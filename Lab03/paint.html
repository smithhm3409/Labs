Different Types of Paint

Acrylic

  • Fast drying
  • Easiest for beginners
  • Waterproof once it is dry

Oil

  • Rather than drying, they oxidize and harden
  • Hardest to work with, but can be rewarding at the end
  • Longest drying time for paint, 6 weeks to a year often times

Watercolor

  • Can come in tubes or cakes and must be activate with water
  • Slower drying, but faster than oil
  • Hard to use, as the water makes it less controllable compared to acrylic

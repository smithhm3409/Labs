Different Drawing Materials

Pencil

  • Graphite pencil is most common, a N. 2 pencil is the most basic form of this
  • Most versatile of drawing media because of the variety
  • On average, a pencil can draw a line for approximately 35 miles!

Pen

  • Roller ball pens with caps are mostly water based, so they can dry out quickly
  • Retractable pens are oil based and dont dry out
  • A basic pen like Bic can do wonders as a beginner
